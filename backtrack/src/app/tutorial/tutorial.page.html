Tutorial

Welcome to the Backtrack

This app is design to help you find any trackers nearby that are possibly being use for malicious intents.

How to scan

To begin the scan, make sure you are standing in the room or location you want to look for trackers and tap the blue button towards the top of the screen.

List of trackers

After you begin scanning, the app will bring up a list of trackers under the button, if there are any nearby.

You can also choose to stop scanning at any point by pressing the red button.

Choosing an individual Tracker

When you choose one of the trackers from the list, you will be taken to this page. This will tell you the distance you are away from tracker. The color of the circle will also show how far you are from it:

If the color is Green, you are within 2 meters

If the color is Yellow, you are within 10 meters

If the color is Red, you are farther than 10 meters

Whitelisting

If you know one of the trackers that was pulled up on the list is yours and it's safe, you can whitelist it so it no longer shows up when you are scanning.

Whitelisted items can be managed from the Settings page.

Resources

When you tap the "Found" button or when you choose to look at the resources in the Resource tab, you will be given links for pages that can give you further advice on what to do next.

Always On Option

This app also allows you to constantly scan for trackers while the app is not opened. This feature will take note if there are any trackers constantly around you throughout the day. If the app detects a tracker after a certain amount of times, you will be sent a notification.

Panic Mode

At the bottom right of the screen will be a Panic button. If you want to obscure what you are doing to nearby people, pressing this button will bring up a cat video that hides the previous screen.

We want you to be safe

You can always come back to the tutorial from the Resources tab
